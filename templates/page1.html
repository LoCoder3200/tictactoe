Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player strategy game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking empty squares with their symbol (X or O). The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The first player to create a line of three wins the game. If all squares are filled without a winner, the game is a draw. Strategy is key: block your opponent's potential lines while trying to create your own winning combination.
